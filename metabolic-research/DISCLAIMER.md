# Disclaimer

**This project is a research and educational resource. It is not medical advice.**

- It is **not a diagnostic tool**, not a screening test, and not a substitute for
  evaluation by a qualified clinician.
- The hazard ratios are **population-level statistical associations**. Many are
  observational, not causal — the data layer flags this per cell, and the
  synthesis (`writeup/summary.md`) discusses where associations are routinely
  over-interpreted.
- Every quantified cell carries a `verification_status` of `verified`,
  `approximate`, or `unverified`. The reference ranges carry the same flag.
  **Treat `approximate` and `unverified` figures as provisional** and confirm
  them against the cited primary sources before relying on them.
- The personal dashboard's "modeled hazard" is a **per-biomarker, relative,
  directional estimate** — it is not a personal risk prediction, not an absolute
  probability, and deliberately not a combined score. Hazard ratios across
  correlated biomarkers cannot be multiplied together.
- Reference ranges are not age- or sex-stratified in this version; results for
  age/sex-dependent markers (fitness, strength, hormones, body composition)
  should be interpreted with that limitation in mind.

**Do not make medical decisions based on this resource.** If you are considering
changes to medication, supplementation, screening, or treatment, consult a
licensed physician who can account for your individual history.
