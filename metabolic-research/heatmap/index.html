Reading the map. Hazard ratios are drawn from the source study and are not all on the same scale — some are per standard deviation, some per quartile contrast, some per fixed unit. The exact metric is shown in every tooltip. Cell intensity encodes effect magnitude as |ln(HR)| so that risk and protective markers of equal strength read alike.

Verification status. Each evidence cell is flagged verified (source study located and figures confirmed), approximate (study identified, exact CI/N not confirmed), or unverified (general domain knowledge). Verify all figures against primary sources before clinical use.
