How risk is modeled. For each biomarker, your value is placed on the population SD scale; the distance from your risk-optimal range is translated through that biomarker's verified per-SD hazard ratio into a modeled hazard relative to being at optimal. This is a per-biomarker estimate, not a diagnosis and not a combined score — hazard ratios across correlated biomarkers cannot be multiplied together. Modeled figures are directional coaching signals; confirm with a clinician.

Not medical advice. A research and educational tool — not a diagnostic device, not a personal risk prediction, and not a substitute for evaluation by a qualified clinician.
